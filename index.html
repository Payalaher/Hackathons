Rounds

Whoever appearing for the hackathon will go through the three rounds in total.

The first round is for the documentationa and UI round in which each team will go through the
documentation phase and finaling the final UI for there consecutive project topic.

After documentation and the final UI, second round is of the Development round in which each team instructed to complete there respective projects.

Then the final round is of the presentation round in which each team will get the 7 minutes each to present there respective project.

And after the careful consideration and the marking scheme, total 3 teams will get selected for the prize
money.
